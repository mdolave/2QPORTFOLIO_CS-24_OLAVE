Leni Robredo: A Legacy of Leadership with Heart

Legislatures Authored and Co-Authored

According to Vote Pilipinas (2020) , Leni Robredo served as the Representative for the 3rd District of Camarines Sur in the 16th Congress from 2013 to 2016. During her single term as a lawmaker, she principally authored 35 bills; three of which were enacted into law.

Republic Act No. 10708: Tax Incentives Management and Transparency Act

This law establishes a system for reporting and managing tax incentives given to businesses. Leni Robredo authored this to promote fiscal accountability and transparency in the grant of tax incentives by developing clear means in analyzing its economic impacts, which will optimize the social benefit of such incentives. Under this law, it is mandated that "all registered business entities are required to file their tax returns and pay their tax liabilities, on or before the deadline as provided under the Internal Revenue Code (NIRC)." (Arellano Law Foundation, n.d.)

Republic Act No. 10922: The Economic and Financial Literacy Act

This law declares the second week of November as Economic and Financial Literacy Week annually. This is crucial in raising national awareness about economic and financial literacy by mandating government agenicies and youth organizations to conduct activities or integrate financial topics into the academoc curriculum. In our country deeply saddened by poverty, fostering sound financial decisions which improve the economy should be of utmost importance. Under this Republic Act, it is written that "the State recognizes the growth potential of the country through a financially literate people." (Manila Bulletin, 2016)

House Bill No. 19: Full Disclosure Bill

Robredo filed this on her first day as a lawmaker. Should this bill have been passed into law, all national and local government agencies would have to publicly disclose all information on budget allocations and financial transactions. Under this bill, all government agencies must adhere "instead of merely issuing a statement of income and expenses." (Senate of the Philippines Legislative Bureau, n.d)
